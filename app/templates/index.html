How to Play Pips

What is Pips? It's a daily puzzle where you fill a grid by placing dominoes. Each domino has two halves, and each half shows 0 to 6 dots (called "pips").

The goal: Place all the given dominoes onto the grid so that every rule is satisfied. Rules might say things like "these cells must add up to 8" or "these cells must all be different."

Reading the solution: In the solved grid below, cells that share the same color are the two halves of one domino. The number in each cell is how many pips are on that half.
